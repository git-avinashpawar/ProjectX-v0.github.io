Magic Mirror : An AI Smart Mirror

Project Information

  • Category: Web design, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning
  • Project date: 21 May, 2020
  • Project URL: https://github.com/git-avinashpawar/AI-Smart-Mirror
  • Python Libraries Used: SpeechRecognition 3.8.1, wolframalpha 3.0.1, JSON, PyAudio 0.2.11, Wikipedia, requests2 2.16.0, Eel

Technologies used : Python3, Java, Android, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap5, Firebase, Git, Github

View on Github

Introducing Magic Mirror, an innovative project powered by Raspberry Pi. This smart mirror combines functionality and style, offering a unique interactive experience with integrated apps like weather updates, music playback, news updates, and a voice-activated assistant.

Developed as a final year (Semester VIII) project for a BTech in Computer Science and Technology, with a team of four.

Technical Specifications:

  • Raspberry Pi: The Magic Mirror utilizes the Raspberry Pi platform, providing the computing power and flexibility required for its smart features.
  • Smart Mirror Interface: The mirror itself is transformed into a dynamic display, showcasing a variety of information such as the current weather, personalized news headlines, and upcoming calendar events.
  • Weather App: Stay informed about the weather conditions with real-time updates displayed directly on the mirror. Know what to expect before stepping out.
  • Music App: Enjoy your favorite tunes while getting ready for the day. The integrated music app lets you stream music and control playback right from the mirror.
  • News App: Stay updated with the latest news headlines from your favorite sources. The Magic Mirror's news app provides curated news snippets to keep you informed.
  • Voice-Activated Assistant: Interact with the Magic Mirror using voice commands. Ask questions, get personalized information, and control various features hands-free.
  • Android App: The Magic Mirror project includes an accompanying Android app that serves as a convenient remote control. Seamlessly manage the mirror's functions and settings from your Android device.

The Magic Mirror project combines technology and design to create a seamless blend of functionality and aesthetics. It serves as an all-in-one solution for accessing important information while adding a touch of modern sophistication to any space.

Whether you place it in your bedroom, hallway, or living room, the Magic Mirror is sure to become a conversation starter and an invaluable addition to your daily routine. Experience the future of smart home technology with the Magic Mirror powered by Raspberry Pi, and control it conveniently through the accompanying Android app.
